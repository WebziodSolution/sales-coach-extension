WHY TAKE ACTION NOW

1. What’s changed recently that makes solving this a priority now?
2. In what ways is this challenge affecting your day-to-day operations or business performance?
3. If no action is taken, what impact do you expect over the next few months?

BUSINESS IMPACT & VALUE

4. What business results would you expect to see if this issue were successfully resolved?
5. Where is this problem creating the biggest impact today — revenue, efficiency, time, or operational cost?
6. Which KPIs or performance indicators are most important to improve through this initiative?
7. From leadership’s perspective, what outcomes would define success for this project?

BUDGET & OWNERSHIP

8. Who is responsible for making the final investment decision on projects like this?

INTERNAL CHAMPION

9. Who inside the organization is feeling this pain point most strongly?
10. Who would likely lead or advocate for this initiative internally?

HOW A SOLUTION WILL BE EVALUATED

11. What factors will matter most when comparing and selecting a solution — functionality, integration, security, ROI, support, or something else?

DECISION-MAKING PROCESS

12. Can you walk me through how a decision like this typically moves from evaluation to approval, including the people involved?

PROCUREMENT & APPROVALS

13. What internal reviews or approvals — such as procurement, legal, compliance, or security — should we expect along the way?
14. What usually causes delays in getting a purchase approved and finalized?

CURRENT SITUATION

15. How are you currently handling this process or challenge today?
16. Are you evaluating any other vendors or approaches at the moment?
17. What concerns or risks would need to be addressed for you to feel confident moving forward?

MOVING FORWARD

18. Based on today’s discussion, what do you see as the best next step?
19. What additional information would help your team move this evaluation forward?
20. Who else should be included in the next discussion to keep the process moving efficiently?
